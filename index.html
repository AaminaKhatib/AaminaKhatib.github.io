Projects

I have collaborated in a team of four on a project within the scope of my Third Year project which saw the team win the 31st Annual Projects Day hosted by the Academy of Computer Science and Software Engineering (ACSSE) at the University of Johanneburg in 2017.

STAPLE

Student, Teacher and Parent Linked Environment

STAPLE was the first system of its kind which combined all administrative procedures required by a School with a communicator. The biggest advantage of having such a system in place is that it allows for personalised communication between the School, Teacher, Student and Parent. Our system features included receiving marks in real time, a personalised calendar for Parents, an attendance system for Teachers, real time notifications and effective reporting for Parents, Teachers and School Administrator. Unfortunately, the project was never made live.
